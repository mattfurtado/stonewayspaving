About StoneWays Paving

StoneWays Inc. is a hardscape company servicing central & eastern Massachusetts. We are certified ICPI (Interlocking Concrete Paving Institute) installers of concrete paver driveways, pool decks, patios, walkways, outdoor fire pits, and retaining walls (both fieldstone & interlocking).

With over 10 years of experience working for other companies the owner, Chris Harper, decided to start StoneWays Inc. in 2006. With the combination of being a certified ICPI installer and the many years of experience in the industry, we know what it takes to get the job done right the first time.

StoneWays Inc. is fully licensed (MA. Home Improvement Contractors license # 154004) and insured (both general liability & workers compensation).

Homeowners look for two things when it comes to outdoor living spaces…they want it to look beautiful and last for a lifetime. That’s why at StoneWays Inc. we work closely with each customer to ensure that you are 100% satisified when the job is complete. We use a blend of colors to complement your home’s beauty and surrounding landscaping.

StoneWays Inc. treats your property like it was our own, and we take a lot of pride in what we do to make sure that you get the quality and craftsmanship that expect and deserve. Our work clearly speaks for itself…just ask any of our customers and they will tell you about their experience with StoneWays Inc.
